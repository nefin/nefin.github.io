## 3. Homepage

### Mission statement (body text)
> NEFIN is the Brazilian Center for Research in Financial Economics of the University of São Paulo. The Center was created by researchers of the Department of Economics.
>
> Our area of research is Financial Economics: asset pricing, derivative pricing, corporate finance, risk management and portfolio allocation.
>
> We make available for free download data sets and variables that are typically used by academics and practitioners, such as Brazilian risk factors and stock portfolios. The data are updated on a regular basis.

### 5.2 Volatility Index (IVol-BR)
**URL:** `/data/volatility_index.html`  
**Data file:** `/data/IVol-BR.csv`  
Interactive Chart.js chart rendering the IVol-BR time series. Methodology: implied, forward-looking volatility index for the Brazilian stock market, combining international methodology with adjustments for low liquidity of IBOVESPA options. Decomposed into variance premium and expected variance; time-varying risk aversion coefficient extracted.

## 10. Technical notes for redesign

- **Google Analytics ID:** G-60RY20MC3F (keep)
- **Charts:** Chart.js (v3.7.0) + D3.js (v5.7.0) used for interactive time-series charts on data pages
- **Data format:** CSV files in `resources/` subdirectories; some older XLS files also present
- **No backend / CMS:** Pure static HTML — no templating. All nav is duplicated per page.
- **Current theme:** ProBootstrap Enlight (Bootstrap 3) — to be replaced entirely
- **Gulp pipeline:** `gulp sass` for SCSS, `gulp scripts` for JS bundling
- **Custom CSS entry point:** `css/custom.css` (always last in load order)
- **Python tooling:** `python_general_scripts/converting_xls_to_csv.ipynb` for XLS→CSV conversion
