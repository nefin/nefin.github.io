Center for Research in Financial Economics of the University of São Paulo

NEFIN is the Brazilian Center for Research in Financial Economics of the University of São Paulo. The Center was created by researchers of the Department of Economics.

Our area of research is Financial Economics: asset pricing, derivative pricing, corporate finance, risk management and portfolio allocation.

We make available for free download data sets and variables that are typically used by academics and practitioners, such as Brazilian risk factors and stock portfolios. The data are updated on a regular basis.
